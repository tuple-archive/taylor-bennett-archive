Navigating a new world

We work with clients across almost every sector and geography, successfully placing senior corporate affairs professionals with some of the most influential companies and organisations in the world.

Emerging global challenges, polarising politics and the rise of social activism ask more questions of every organisation. It’s a new world. Whatever your take on it, responding with purpose, creativity and clarity has never been more important. We can help you find the right people to rise to that responsibility.

As the landscape of corporate affairs continues to change, and as stakeholders and audiences grow, we’re here to help you interpret the shifts and what they mean for your organisation. Now and in the years to come.

Our Network

An international network

Complementing our extensive network across EMEA, with our sister companies Heyman Associates in the United States and GK in the DACH markets, we can deliver truly global access.

Together we make quite a team. Between us we meet more than 2,000 professionals in corporate affairs every year. We have been established in EMEA since 1982, the US since 1989, and have also managed searches and developed networks in Asia. No other search organisation combines such depth and international reach.

Heyman Associates

Heyman Associates was founded by William C. Heyman in New York in 1989. With more than two decades of disciplined work in communications search across industries and geographies, it has earned the trust of both clients and candidates. This trust, in turn, has seen the organisation become one of the most respected thought leaders in our industry and a business we’ve been proud to partner with for over 15 years.

heymanassociates.com

GK Consulting

Based in Frankfurt, Germany, GK has been the leading DACH region executive search firm for the corporate affairs industry since 1991. Their years of experience and deep insights give them unrivalled knowledge of the entire field of communications, public affairs, investor relations and marketing. We have worked with the team at GK for 20 years.
